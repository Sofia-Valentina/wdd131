La Gran Sabana, Venezuela

I was born in La Gran Sabana, a beautiful and unique natural region in southeastern Venezuela. La Gran Sabana is part of the Guiana Shield, one of the oldest geological formations on Earth, known for its vast plains, dramatic tabletop mountains called tepuis, and breathtaking waterfalls. This region is home to rich biodiversity, with many plant and animal species found nowhere else. La Gran Sabana is also part of Canaima National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and features incredible landscapes that attract visitors from around the world.
